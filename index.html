About China Quest

China Quest is a transformative educational programme designed for students aged 14-18, offering immersive experiences across China's most dynamic cities. Through hands-on learning, cultural exploration, and innovation challenges, students develop global competencies while discovering their future potential.

We combine structured learning with authentic exploration, enabling students to engage with China's rapid development, rich heritage, and diverse cultures in meaningful ways.

Our Story

Since 2005, Miles Minds has been helping people discover China—not as tourists, but as learners. What began as a consultancy supporting international organisations has evolved into a dedicated educational platform, designing immersive experiences for students, professionals, and institutions.

China Quest was born from a simple belief: young people deserve to understand the world they're inheriting. China is no longer distant or abstract—it's central to global economics, innovation, and culture. Our programmes bridge the gap between classroom theory and real-world understanding.

Every programme we design reflects our core values: curiosity, respect, and depth. We don't simplify China; we help students engage with its complexity. We don't just show sites; we facilitate meaningful encounters with ideas, people, and systems that shape our interconnected world.
